## Persona Template

For each user type, document:
- **Name & Role** - Give them a memorable name and title
- **Goals** - What they want to accomplish
- **Pain Points** - What frustrates them
- **Technical Comfort** - How tech-savvy are they?
- **Primary Tasks** - What do they do most often?
- **Success Metrics** - How do we know they're successful?

## Using Personas in Development

**When creating features:**
1. Ask: "Which persona benefits from this?"
2. Reference their goals and pain points
3. Ensure the feature matches their technical comfort level
4. Verify it supports their primary tasks

**When prioritizing work:**
- Features for primary personas take priority
- Consider impact across multiple personas
- Balance needs of different user types

**When designing UI/UX:**
- Match complexity to technical comfort level
- Prioritize workflows for primary tasks
- Use language that resonates with the persona
